What exams does Pratham Test Prep prepare students for?

Pratham Test Prep offers coaching for entrance exams such as CUET, IPMAT, NMIMS-NPAT, SET, CLAT, AILET, BBA/BMS entrances, Mass Communication entrances, Liberal Arts, and various university-specific tests.

Are classes offered online or offline?

Pratham Test Prep provides both online and classroom learning options. Students can choose whichever suits their schedule and learning style.

Are mock tests included in the program?

Absolutely. Full-length mock tests + sectional tests are part of every program to track progress and boost exam readiness.
